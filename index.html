About

Valerie Sirenko believes stories can change people's lives.

A speculative fiction writer living in the Bay Area, California, Valerie has written two novels and numerous short stories. Her most recent publication, "Just Listed: Hardwood Floors and a Generous Curse," is available now in Orion's Belt. She is an alum of the Futurescapes Writers Workshop and a Codex member. In her academic work, she taught literature and writing at the University of Texas at Austin and Seattle Pacific University. Her scholarship and book reviews have appeared in journals such as American Literature . She received her Ph.D. in English in 2019.

Fiction

Selected Work

Echoing Howl

This anthropomorphic fantasy novel about wolves with magical powers shows us we are nothing without our packs. Ryka, a scrappy loner, needs to find a pack before winter or starve, but she was born without access to the elemental powers necessary for survival. Can she turn her weaknesses into strengths? The story's dramatic landscapes are inspired by the author's many visits to National Parks like Acadia, Yosemite, and the North Cascades. It will appeal to fans of Redwall, His Dark Materials , and Watership Down.

Break A Few Eggs

In this cozy mystery, a baker who is allergic to eggs must uncover who poisoned her cupcakes before her cafe goes out of business. Featuring futuristic plants, a simmering romance, an enemies-to-allies friendship, and an adorable collie, it will appeal to fans of Emily Henry, Sangu Mandanna, and Misha Popp.
